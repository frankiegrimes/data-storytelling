Understanding

What is defined as an understanding of the transgender community?

How aware are you of the terms that apply to your gender? You’ve probably heard of the term transgender before but what does it actually mean? That’s what the aim of Transcend is, to help inform you through the complexities of the transgender community and your perceptions towards them. A good place to start would be an understanding of what it means to be cisgender.

We asked 227 Irish people aged 18-55 for their definition of the term 'cisgender', let's see how yours compares.

Generational Disparity

Digital Natives and Digital Immigrants

Has the internet created a generational disparity? Otherwise known as the “Digital Divide” between those that were born before 1980 and those that were born after. This divide suggests that those born after the internet, otherwise known as “Digital Natives” have an inherent connection with the internet that is fostered throughout their lives that the the “Digital Immigrants” cannot catch up to.

Appendix of data for reference

Survey conducted in 2016. 227 respondents participated in this purposive quantitative survey in order to gauge their awareness of the transgender community and how this awareness was formed. The results from this survey are not representative of the population of Ireland and cannot be used to make generalisations or sweeping claims about views towards the transgender community. The sampling methodology for data collection used non-problematic methods such as critical case sampling, volunteer sampling and convenience sampling.
